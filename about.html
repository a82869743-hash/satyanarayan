The Beginning

A Scoop of Dream, A Sip of Passion

Satyanarayan Ice Cream started as a small, passionate family project rooted in a love for quality desserts. Our founder grew up watching street vendors create magical ice creams, and dreamed of creating something premium — where every customer gets the freshest, most indulgent experience possible.

We opened our first counter with just 6 flavors and one soda machine. Word spread fast. Our commitment to using only real fruits, no artificial colors, and hand-scooping every order set us apart from day one.

Today, Satyanarayan Ice Cream serves hundreds of customers daily with 28+ menu items across 5 categories — and we're still growing, still experimenting, and still serving every scoop with the same love as the very first one.
